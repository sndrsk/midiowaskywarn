Protecting life and property through integrity and collaboration

The Mid-Iowa Skywarn Association (MISA) is an organization comprised of volunteers from the general public who are passionate about spotting hazardous weather to provide the National Weather Service and other agencies detailed "ground truth" information that benefits communities with critical, accurate, and timely weather warning information.

Get involved!

NWS encourages anyone with an interest in public service and access to communication, such as amateur radio, to join the Skywarn program. Training is typically held February through April.
